Tennis Court

It established in 1949

You won't believe how cool our school tennis court is! It's like a mini sports place right here in our school. When you walk by, you can see people playing tennis with rackets and hitting that little ball back and forth. The court is all green with lines on it, and there's a net in the middle. It's like a challenge to get the ball over the net and make sure it bounces in the right spot on the other side. It's not as easy as it looks, but it's super fun. Sometimes during recess, my friends and I go to the court. We take turns hitting the ball and trying to see who can hit it the farthest. And when we actually hit it over the net, it feels like a big win! We're not pros, but we're getting better each time we play. There's this one teacher who knows a lot about tennis, and sometimes they come and show us some tricks. They teach us how to hold the racket and how to stand so we can hit the ball just right. It's like having a secret coach. I really like the tennis court because it's a place where we can have fun and learn at the same time. Maybe one day, I'll be really good at tennis and play like those players on TV. But for now, I'm just enjoying playing with my friends and getting better bit by bit.
